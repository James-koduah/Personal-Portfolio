What Project Am I Currently Working On?

Outside of my main work, I'm taking on a project of building a Point of Sale (POS) app that's targeted at local Ghanaian businesses. The main goal is to make cashless payment easier, tapping into the power of Mobile Money—a payment method that's taken off here.

But here's where I add some spice: Image detection and AI voice assistant at the storefront. Here's my draft — Every few seconds (To save processing power), capture and process image/video frames, use people and face detection, and then interact with customers.

The app will have to adapt to different situations as they happen. It's all about bringing some futuristic tech into the everyday flow of business and creating practical but cool experiences. I'm a bit worried about privacy and data protection but hey! It's AI Magic.
